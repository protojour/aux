Aux

“Power is an abstraction, use it for good, not evil”
Aux is an abstraction framework made to simplify and streamline your testing and integration with devices and software services.

Aux could be used for.

  • Automation
  • Testing
  • Deployment & configuration
  • Integration middleware

Scripting

Scripting is the practice of making routine work simpler, faster and repeatable.

It is a way of documenting and sharing knowledge about routines to avoid "the guy who knew quit" problems.

It let's you streamline the integration testing of your webservice APIs.

Scripting just makes the lazy man's life more bareable, so go ahead start scripting your life and pipelines today!

Testing

Automate your testing using Aux scripts.

So no matter if you are writing your first testscripts, or mastering some advanced integration scenario, aux is made for you!

Integration

Using aux to integrate with your system device or system service, can be done through creating a new plugin.

Whether for keeping in-house implementations of your systems or making your system available for testers and integrators by making your plugin public, you should start by reading how to integrate your systems.
